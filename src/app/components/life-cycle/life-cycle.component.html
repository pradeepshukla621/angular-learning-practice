Angular LifeCycle

Order Hook Description
1 constructor() Class instantiation. Good for dependency injection, avoid side effects here (This is not a angular lifecycle).
2 ngOnChanges() "Called before ngOnInit() and whenever input properties change. (Only if @Input() is used.)
3 ngOnInit() Called once after the first ngOnChanges(). Best for initialization logic.
4 ngDoCheck() Custom change detection, runs with every change detection cycle.
5 ngAfterContentInit() Called once after Angular projects external content into the component.
6 ngAfterContentChecked() Called after every check of projected content.
7 ngAfterViewInit() Called once after the component’s view (and child views) has been initialized.
8 ngAfterViewChecked() Called after every check of the component’s view (and child views).
9 ngOnDestroy() Cleanup logic before the component is removed (unsubscribe, detach handlers, etc).
